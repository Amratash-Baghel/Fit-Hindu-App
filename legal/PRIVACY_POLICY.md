## 4. How and why we use your data

> **[LAWYER REVIEW LR-10]** The purposes below are drafted to be specific enough to satisfy DPDP's itemized-notice requirement while covering the founder's direction to use data "as much as possible to Herbal Deck's advantage" within what is disclosed and consented to (`docs/idea.md` "The data advantage," confirmed in conversation 2026-07-15). Please review: (a) whether each purpose is specific enough, (b) whether the marketing/CRM purposes need their own separate, granular consent toggle distinct from the core-service consent, since DPDP disfavors bundled all-or-nothing consent.

We use your data for the following purposes:

1. **Operating the app** — creating your account, running the onboarding questionnaire, assigning you a plan, and delivering the workout, diet, meditation, jap, and devotional content you request.
2. **Personalization** — tailoring your plan and content to your stated goal, diet type, workout preference, level, and (if chosen) devotional preference.
3. **Progress tracking** — calculating and showing your streaks and activity history.
4. **Internal business improvement** — understanding, in aggregate, how our audience uses the app (which programs are popular, where people drop off) to improve our content and product.
5. **Herbal Deck customer relationship purposes** — if you contact Herbal Deck's customer support or sales team, or if Herbal Deck's team reaches out to you, we may use your goal, diet preference, and activity/adherence data to give that team relevant context, so you don't have to repeat yourself. See §5 for how this data flow is scoped.
6. **Marketing communications** (only if you separately opt in) — telling you about Herbal Deck products or offers that may be relevant to your goals.
7. **Advertising audiences** (only if you separately opt in, and not currently active — see `THIRD_PARTY_PROCESSORS.md` "Roadmap") — using your data, in aggregate or via privacy-preserving matching, to build advertising audiences on platforms like Meta or Google, so our ads reach people with similar goals more efficiently.
8. **Legal compliance** — responding to legal requests and meeting our obligations under Indian law.

We do not use your data for any purpose beyond what is listed here without asking for your consent again.

## 8. Security

We use the following measures, as actually implemented in the app:

- All data is stored in Supabase's managed Postgres database, protected by row-level security (RLS) policies that restrict every table so a user can only read and write their own data (`supabase/migrations/0002` through `0009`).
- No administrative "master key" is used by our admin panel — Herbal Deck staff access is governed by the same row-level security rules, checked against an admin allow-list (`admin/lib/supabase/server.ts`, `supabase/migrations/0002_identity_profiles.sql:19-25`).
- Authentication (sign-in, password/credential handling) is delegated to Supabase's authentication service rather than handled by our own code.
- Video and audio content is served from a separate CDN provider (Bunny.net) and is never stored alongside your personal data.

We describe only what is actually implemented — we do not claim additional protections we have not verified.

> **[LAWYER REVIEW LR-11]** The app's design specifies an "18+ gate; under-18 blocked" (`docs/specs/onboarding-questionnaire.md:30`), but as of this draft **no code enforces this** — the age question itself has not been built yet (only the language-choice screen exists). Recommend the gate still be prioritized as an early post-launch (or pre-launch, if feasible) item, since an unenforced age claim is a real gap the moment the app has real users.
>
> **[LAWYER REVIEW LR-21]** Separately, the founder raised lowering the minimum age to 13+ in the same conversation. Because DPDP Act 2023 defines anyone under 18 as a "child" — triggering verifiable parental consent and a ban on behavioral tracking/targeted advertising directed at children — the founder's decision (2026-07-15) was to **keep 18+ as the actual floor for v1** and treat 13+ as a future consideration only, contingent on building parental-consent infrastructure first. This policy is drafted on the 18+ basis. Note for engineering: the current `age_band` enum (`supabase/migrations/0001_extensions_enums_helpers.sql:11`) only has bands starting at `18_25` and has no 13-17 option, consistent with this decision — do not add one without revisiting this section first.

If we learn that we have collected data from someone under 18 without appropriate consent, we will delete it.
